4. Merriam-Webster Word of the Day: Subscribing to Merriam-Webster’s Word of the Day email or using their mobile app can help you learn new words, their meanings, and proper usage.

5. Spellzone: This website provides interactive spelling lessons and activities, including word lists, games, and tests, to improve your spelling skills at different levels.

6. Anki: Anki is a flashcard app that uses spaced repetition algorithms to help you memorize and retain vocabulary words effectively.

7. Words with Friends: Playing word games like Words with Friends can be a fun and engaging way to expand your vocabulary and improve your spelling.

Remember, regular practice and exposure to new words are key to enhancing your vocabulary and spelling abilities.
