User: Can you explain how GPS navigation systems work?

Assistant: GPS navigation systems work by using a network of satellites which provide precise location information. These satellites orbit the Earth and continuously transmit signals to GPS receivers, such as those found in smartphones or dedicated GPS devices. The GPS receiver then calculates the exact position of the device by measuring the time it takes for the signals to reach it from multiple satellites. This information is used to determine the user’s location and provide turn-by-turn directions to their desired destination.

However, it’s worth mentioning that the environmental impact of EVs also depends on how the electricity used to charge them is generated.
